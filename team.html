Our Team

Dedicated to understanding neural mechanisms of social behavior and neuropsychiatric disorders

Principal Investigator

Guang-Wei Zhang, Ph.D.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Zhang's research focuses on understanding the neural mechanisms underlying social behavior and how these mechanisms are disrupted in neuropsychiatric disorders. The laboratory uses cutting-edge techniques in neuroscience to investigate neural circuits controlling social impairment, with the goal of identifying novel therapeutic targets.
