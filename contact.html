Visiting Our Lab

By Car

From I-95/I-64:

  • Take Exit 74C (Broad Street)
  • Turn right onto N 10th Street
  • Turn left onto E Marshall Street
  • Sanger Hall will be on your right

Parking: Visitor parking is available in the D Deck (8th and Marshall Streets) or A Deck (12th and Marshall Streets)

By Air

Richmond International Airport (RIC) is approximately 15 minutes from campus.

  • Taxi/Uber/Lyft available at airport
  • Rental cars available
  • Airport shuttle services to downtown Richmond

Public Transportation

GRTC Transit System serves the VCU Medical Campus:

  • Multiple bus routes stop near Sanger Hall
  • Pulse BRT line connects to main campus
  • Check GRTC website for schedules
